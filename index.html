Current product

TapReply — AI Review Manager for local business.

TapReply is the company’s proprietary B2B application and primary product under development.

TapReply B2B SaaS

TapReply helps businesses process customer reviews faster by generating professional, brand-safe response drafts with AI. The product is designed for local service companies, hospitality, retail, clinics, and other operators that rely on public reputation.

  • AI-assisted review reply generation for daily reputation management.
  • Mobile-first workflow for owners, managers, and small teams.
  • Cloud backend with structured data storage and API-based integrations.
  • Roadmap focused on multi-location support, analytics, templates, and team workflows.
